Siyuan Yang

Wallenberg–NTU Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow

I am a Wallenberg–NTU Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow, currently hosted at KTH Royal Institute of Technology and working with Prof. Hedvig Kjellström. Before joining KTH, I conducted postdoctoral research at the Rapid-Rich Object Search Lab, Nanyang Technological University.

I received my Ph.D. and M.Sc. degrees from Nanyang Technological University, where my Ph.D. research was supervised by Prof. Alex C. Kot and Assoc. Prof. Shijian Lu, and my B.Eng. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology. My research focuses on human-centered visual understanding, including video and skeleton action recognition, 3D human motion understanding, and human pose estimation.
